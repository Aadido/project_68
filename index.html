School campus

Was established in 1961

The Indian High School is a CBSE-affiliated Indian school in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. It was established in 1961 to provide education to the children of Indian expatriates and was the first expatriate educational institution in the UAE.



School auditorium

Was established in 1961

As an auditorium, this 7,700 square-metre hall can be transformed using tiered retractable seating for 2,500 with an additional 2,000 seats on the flat. Alternatively, you can use this space as a banquet set up, catering for up to 3,800 guests.

  • New school canteen

    In the heart of the educational hub, IHS boasts a vibrant and bustling canteen that serves as the social epicenter for students. Nestled within the school premises, the IHS canteen is not merely a place to satiate hunger but a dynamic space fostering camaraderie and cultural exchange. The aroma of diverse cuisines wafts through the air as students gather during breaks to share laughter and forge lasting friendships. From savory snacks to nutritious meals, the canteen caters to a spectrum of tastes, accommodating both local and international palates. Its lively atmosphere mirrors the diverse student body at IHS, fostering a sense of unity and inclusivity. Run by a dedicated team, the IHS canteen is not just a culinary destination but an integral part of the school's identity, promoting a healthy and interactive environment that transcends the conventional notion of school dining. It encapsulates the spirit of IHS, where nourishment extends beyond the plate to cultivate a rich tapestry of connections among students.

  • Online attendance system

    IHS (International High School) has embraced technological innovation with its state-of-the-art online attendance system, revolutionizing the traditional method of tracking student presence. The system seamlessly integrates cutting-edge software and user-friendly interfaces, streamlining the attendance process for both students and faculty. Implemented to enhance efficiency and accuracy, the online attendance system at IHS allows students to mark their attendance with a few clicks, reducing the administrative burden on teachers. Real-time data updates enable prompt identification of attendance trends and patterns, facilitating timely interventions when necessary. Moreover, parents benefit from this system as they gain access to their child's attendance records instantly, fostering transparent communication between the school and home. The IHS online attendance system exemplifies the institution's commitment to leveraging technology for educational excellence, ensuring a smooth and accountable attendance tracking process that aligns with the school's progressive approach to learning and administration.
